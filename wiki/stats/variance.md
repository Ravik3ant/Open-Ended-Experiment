# Variance (`s^2`)

## Definition

Sample variance measures average squared deviation around the sample mean.

## Formula

`s^2 = (1/(N-1)) * sum((x_i - mu)^2)`

## Why `N-1`?

Using `N-1` (Bessel correction) makes sample variance an unbiased estimator of population variance.

## Importance

- Quantifies fluctuation energy.
- Enters Fano factor (`variance/mean`).
