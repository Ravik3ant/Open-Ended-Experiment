# Fano Factor (`F`)

## Definition

Variance-to-mean ratio.

## Formula

`F = variance / mean`

## Interpretation

- `F ~ 1`: Poisson-like process
- `F > 1`: over-dispersion (extra variability)
- `F < 1`: under-dispersion

## Importance

Compact scalar diagnostic of how close data dispersion is to Poisson expectation.
