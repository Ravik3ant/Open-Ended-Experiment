# Relative Counting Error (`1/sqrt(mu)`)

## Definition

Approximate fractional Poisson uncertainty of counts.

## Formula

For Poisson counts:

- absolute SD: `sqrt(mu)`
- relative SD: `sqrt(mu) / mu = 1/sqrt(mu)`

## Importance

- Explains why longer counting times give better fractional precision.
- Even if absolute SD grows, fractional uncertainty drops.
