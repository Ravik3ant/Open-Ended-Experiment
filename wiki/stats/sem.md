# Standard Error of Mean (SEM)

## Definition

Uncertainty of the sample mean estimate.

## Formula

`SEM = s / sqrt(N)`

## Derivation sketch

If independent repeated measurements have variance `sigma^2`, then mean of `N` samples has variance `sigma^2 / N`. Replacing `sigma` by sample SD `s` gives SEM estimate.

## Importance

- Smaller SEM means more precise mean estimate.
- Demonstrates why increasing sample size improves reliability.
