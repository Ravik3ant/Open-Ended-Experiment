# Mean (`mu`)

## Definition

The arithmetic average count over `N` measurements.

## Formula

`mu = (1/N) * sum(x_i)`

## Derivation intuition

The sample mean is the maximum-likelihood estimator for Poisson `lambda`.

## Importance

- Central intensity estimate of the decay process.
- Input to expected Poisson spread `sqrt(mu)`.
- Used in expected histogram frequencies.
